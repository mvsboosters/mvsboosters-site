Monta Vista Speech Boosters

The MVS Boosters is a parent and community-led nonprofit organization with a single, vital mission: to ensure the students of the Monta Vista Speech team have the resources they need to excel. We handle the fundraising, financial management, and volunteer coordination that allows the coaches and students to focus on what they do best: competing, learning, and growing.

As a registered 501(c)(3) organization, your contributions are tax-deductible. Our operations are funded entirely by the generosity of parents, alumni, and community members like you.

Your Support in Action

100% of our funding comes from donations. Here's what your contributions make possible:

Professional Coaching

Funding stipends for experienced coaches who provide invaluable guidance and mentorship to our students.

Tournament Fees

Covering registration costs for local, state, and national tournaments, ensuring every student has the chance to compete.

Travel & Logistics

Arranging and subsidizing travel and lodging for out-of-town competitions, a major expense for the team.

Team Resources

Providing essential educational materials, software subscriptions, and funding for team-building events.
